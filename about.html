About Gurjas Industries

Gurjas Industries, located in Ambernath, Thane, Maharashtra, is a well-established Proprietorship firm founded in 2004. Specializing in Heavy engineering work including precision, Plano milling, VTL, and Horizontal Boring Machines machining job work, we have earned a reputation for excellence in industrial machinery manufacturing.



Equipped with state-of-the-art machinery such as Planno Millers (Sagar Make), Boring Machines (Karam Make), VTL (Western Bennett), and Milling Machines (Huron Make), we offer comprehensive machining solutions. Our capabilities include X, Y, Z Axis Traveling ranging from 2000mm to 6000mm, Spindle Diameter of 110mm, and Chuck Diameter of 64 inches. At Gurjas Industries, we take pride in our commitment to customized fabrication and complete job and component development based on drawings or specific customer requirements. Over the years, we have successfully executed diverse projects, including stone cutting machines, chemical mixer plants, line polish machines, machine maintenance, and steel plant components and machining job work.



Our expertise extends to manufacturing All kinds of Machine Tools & SPM Machine Parts as per Drawing/Specifications, offering complete job development with any material across Maharashtra for various industries. With a focus on precision and reliability, Gurjas Industries stands as a trusted partner in the realm of industrial machinery and machining services.
